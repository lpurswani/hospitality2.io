About

Five Star Cafe

Run by the culinary arts students at Frehold High School, the Five Star Cafe is the work of the Culinary Arts/Hospitality Management Program. These students learn, under our trained chefs, the basics of the culinary industry and as they progress through the years work practice holpitality skills in different areas of their own restaurant.
